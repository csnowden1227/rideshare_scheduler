Welcome to Chauffeur Deluxe Rideshare App

Use this page after sign-up to finish your account setup, connect your business tools, and launch your booking system the right way.

This is the client onboarding page for operators who signed up for the app. It is separate from partner invite onboarding, which is only for dispatch partners joining your farm-out network.

What To Do Next

Your account setup happens inside the setup wizard. That wizard is where you connect Google Maps, your CRM private integration token, payment provider, fleet calendars, pricing, service area, and branding.

Step 1 Confirm your CRM sub-account is active and make note of the location ID tied to your rideshare account.
Step 2 Open the setup wizard and complete business branding, maps, payment provider, CRM webhook, pricing, fleet, and service area settings.
Step 3 Save the wizard, test a booking, confirm the map works, and verify your CRM and payment integrations are connected.
Step 4 After launch, return to the Dispatch Network page if you want to invite partner operators and manage farm-out split settings.
