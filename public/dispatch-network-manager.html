Dispatch Network Settings

Invite Partners and Set Your Farm-Out Split

Use this page to invite new accepting partners into your dispatch network and set the percentage each partner receives when they fulfill one of your rides. Stripe processing fees are deducted first, billed to the farming-out operator, and the remaining net amount is split using the percentages you save here.

The clean MVP rule is already baked in: the accepting partner percentage is the editable value, your percentage is calculated automatically, and the split model stays net after Stripe fee.

How the Split Works

The source operator controls the agreement. You set the accepting partner percentage, the app calculates your remaining percentage, and each dispatch offer can inherit that agreement automatically.

Split Model: Net after Stripe fee
Stripe Fee Charged To: Farming-out operator
Editable Field: Accepting partner percentage
Calculated Field: Your percentage
