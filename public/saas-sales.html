What Happens After Purchase

Buying the SaaS is step one. After that, the operator still needs to log in at app.crmonesource.com and finish setup so the app can actually run for their business.

1. Log In Sign in to app.crmonesource.com using the CRM account tied to the purchase.
2. Open the Wizard Use the custom menu link inside CRM to open the setup wizard for the account.
3. Complete Profile and Integrations Connect calendars, API keys, branding, webhook settings, payments, and service area details.
4. Test and Launch Run test bookings, verify workflows, and confirm the correct vehicle calendars and automation are working.
